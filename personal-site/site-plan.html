Overview

Purpose

To supply a small news outlet of my Church Mission. Instead of having a weekly email, I would like to have a website whereI can refer people for basic contact info as a missionary and have some mini-textual answers to general gospel questions like a Q&A.

Audience

My audience is going to my family and friends from home, which ranges for all ranges, so I want to make this website as user-friendly as possible. MY Ausdience will want one place that they can go to in order to get updates from my mission, a basic reference guide (like a Q&A) for how I've been teaching the Gospel, and a gallery page for all my photos
